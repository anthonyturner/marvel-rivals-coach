Build Theory

Utility builds and how they differ from damage, sustain, mobility, and ultimate plans.

Marvel Rivals does not ask you to buy items mid-match. A build is the way you shape a hero around their strongest fight pattern: which cooldowns you protect, which angles you take, what your team plays around, and how you turn one advantage into a won fight.

Core Difference

Utility builds are about control, not raw output.

A damage build asks how quickly you can threaten a target. A sustain build asks how long your team can survive pressure. A mobility build asks where the fight should happen. An ultimate build asks how to create the fight-winning cast. A utility build asks what tool removes the enemy's best option.
