Current Read

Still a top live-service shooter, but no longer in launch-hype orbit.

Marvel Rivals is sitting near Overwatch on Steam concurrency in this snapshot, while still showing stronger top-seller placement and a much larger all-time Steam peak. It trails giants like Counter-Strike 2, Dota 2, and Apex in raw Steam concurrency, which is normal for a hero shooter compared with Steam's biggest evergreen titles.

Important Caveat

Steam is only one slice of the player base.

Marvel Rivals also runs on console and other ecosystems, so Steam numbers are best for trend comparison, not total population. For coaching purposes, the useful signal is whether the game has enough active players, attention, and revenue momentum to keep receiving updates.
